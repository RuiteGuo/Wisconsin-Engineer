2016 Photo Contest



Grand prize: $100 City of Madison Gift Card!

4 runner-ups and various other prizes for other awesome entries!

It's super easy to enter!

  • Go out and take an awesome photo... of anything.
  • Decide what category your photo fits into (Portrait, Landscape, Still Life or Miscellaneous).
  • Emil your photo to wisconsinengineerphotocontest@gmail.com by MARCH 29th!
  • Make sure you include a title for your photo and what category you are entering it into!
  • Patiently wait while the Wisconsin Engineer Magazine staff votes on submissions and picks the winners!
  • After you win (hopefully), pick up a copy of the Wisconsin Engineer Magazine to see your photo!

There are some rules, right?

  • Maximum of three (3) entries per person.
  • You must be a student to enter.
  • If you win, we will notify the same email address you used to submit your photo. You will have 30 days to pick up your prize (we will let you know how) or it may be forfeited.
  • By submitting your photo, you are granting the Wisconsin Engineer Magazine the rights to:
    • use the photograph in any and all forms of media, including but not limited to electronic, digital media and print.
    • publish your name.
    • edit, revise, adapt and crop the photo as necessary.
  • Lastly, you agree that: You are the sole author of the photograph and control all rights for its use. The photograph is original. You have the rights to grant usage of the photograph as stated below. There has been no prior sale, publication or transfer of rights to the photograph. The photograph shall not contain any libelous or scandalous matter. To the best of your knowledge, the photograph's creation, publication and use shall not infringe any copyright, right of privacy or right of publicity, or infringe or violate any right of any third party.

Interested in joining us?

We're always looking for new people to join our staff! It doesn't matter if you're interested in writing, photography, graphic design, social media, or web development - we welcome all interested students to work with the team they prefer!

We have a kickoff meeting at the beginning of each semester to welcome new members and get them working on the right project. All students are welcome - even those not in an Engineering program!
